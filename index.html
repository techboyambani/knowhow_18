What is Know How ?

“ KNOW HOW ” is a biggest skill development festival organised by “ TRAINING & PLACEMENT CELL ”, Birla Vishvakarma Mahavidyalaya College of Engineering. It consists of events designed to bring the best out of our students to provide them with the experience of industrial expert and make them “ Corporate Ready Material ” and a “ Good personality ”.

Vision

To provide pivotal support & develop long lasting relationship with the companies of domestic & global knowledge economy so that such companies visit to recruit talented final year students of our college yearly, with aim to provide bright and rewarding opportunities & career to them for the valuable contributions they make in growth of company after joining the offered jobs.





Mission

To create awareness and to provide experience among students about the actual placement process and to help students to find their own strength and weakness, so that they can improve over a due course of time. To improve their technical skills by conducting technical tests, conducting various practical events to help them improve their communication skills. They will experience an environment similar to corporate environment giving them necessary industrial exposure which will make a brighter future for our students and consequently lead to the increment in the placement of BVM.
